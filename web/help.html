What is the BGCMAP Database?

BGCs are central to microbial specialized metabolism and natural product discovery. The BGCMAP Database was developed to organize biosynthetic information across linked biological layers, from source samples to reconstructed genomes, predicted gene clusters, and clustered families. Rather than presenting BGCs as isolated records, the database emphasizes traceability across Sample, MAG, BGC, and GCF levels.
