What is the BGCMAP Database?

The BGCMAP database (Biosynthetic Gene Clusters from Metagenome-Assembled genomes with Product predictions) organizes biosynthetic information across four interconnected levels: Sample → MAG (Metagenome-Assembled Genome) → BGC (Biosynthetic Gene Cluster) → NP (Natural Product). Each level carries extensive metadata, allowing users to trace from environmental context through taxonomic classification, specific biosynthetic gene clusters, their evolutionary families, and natural product predictions. The database integrates data from published metagenomic studies and includes antiSMASH-derived annotations, NPClassifier natural product predictions, and BiG-SLiCE clustering.
